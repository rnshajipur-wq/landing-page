About Us

Why Choose RNS Warehouses?

Strategically located on NH-77 in Ghoswar, Hajipur, RNS Warehouses offers a state-of-the-art 60,000 sq.ft. boundary wall-enclosed warehousing facility. With premium FM-2 grade flooring, 18-foot clear height ceilings, and advanced infrastructure, we provide optimal storage solutions for businesses of all sizes.
